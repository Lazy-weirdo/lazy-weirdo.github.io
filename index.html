About Me

· · · ✦ · · ·

Started this run early — full-stack web&app development first, then graphic design, then game dev. Along the way I’ve built platforms for a government ministry, sold a logistics system to a startup, worked retail sales and marketing for a global sportswear brands like 361°, and taught programming and computer hardware fundmentals to two engineering departments at a university.

I’m a generalist who digs deep on whatever the project actually needs. Point me at a problem and I’ll figure it out, build it, and document it on the way out.

These days I’m mostly focused on building things for streamers, content creators, and game projects — overlays, bots, stream tools, game prototypes, video editing, branding, and full web platforms. If it needs someone who can think it through, build it, and ship it properly — that’s where I come in.
